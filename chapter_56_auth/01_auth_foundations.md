# 56.1 Authentication and Authorization: Why It Matters

This chapter introduces **Authentication and Authorization** as part of a practical solo-builder stack.

## Why This Chapter Exists

A solo builder needs tools that are:

- fast to learn
- realistic to maintain
- useful in real products
- strong enough for production growth

Authentication and Authorization helps because it supports **login, sessions, tokens, and access control**.

- what problem Authentication and Authorization solves
- when to choose it
- how it fits with the rest of the stack
- what common beginner mistakes to avoid
