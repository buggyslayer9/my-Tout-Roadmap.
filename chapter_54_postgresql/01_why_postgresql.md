# 54.1 PostgreSQL: Why It Matters

This chapter introduces **PostgreSQL** as part of a practical solo-builder stack.

## Why This Chapter Exists

A solo builder needs tools that are:

- fast to learn
- realistic to maintain
- useful in real products
- strong enough for production growth

PostgreSQL helps because it supports **relational database fundamentals for real apps**.

## What You Should Learn Here

- what problem PostgreSQL solves
- when to choose it
- how it fits with the rest of the stack
- what common beginner mistakes to avoid
